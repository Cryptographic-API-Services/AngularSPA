About

Cryptographic API Services is your security team's favorite Cryptography platform. Allowing your security team to view when and what your developers are doing with their cryptography algorithms in real-time. While allowing you to achieve speeds that on average benchmark faster than Microsoft's built in cryptography.

We are currently in a pre-alpha stage with the online platform. However, we provide a C# SDK that interfaces with the RustCrypto and Dalek-Cryptography suite of algorithms, that is totally free to use from a download on Nuget. We are a totally open-source company and encourage security reports and correspondence be sent here.
